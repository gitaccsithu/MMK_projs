# InsightFlow AI — Demo Questions

Use these prompts to benchmark RAG retrieval and answer quality.

## HR & policies

- What is our remote work policy?
- How do I request annual leave?
- How many PTO days do employees get per year?
- What is the hybrid office schedule requirement?
- Summarize the employee handbook code of conduct.

## Security

- Explain the security policy for passwords and MFA.
- How do I report a security incident?
- What are the data classification levels?
- Is VPN required for remote access?

## Engineering

- What are the engineering deployment steps to production?
- What branch naming convention should I use?
- Who needs to approve production deploys?

## Onboarding

- Summarize the onboarding guide for week 1.
- What accounts do new hires receive?
- What is the buddy program?

## Product

- What is InsightFlow AI and what are its key features?
- What document types does the ingestion pipeline support?
- What roles exist in InsightFlow (member vs admin)?

## Incident response

- What are the SEV levels and response times?
- When is a postmortem required?
- How do I escalate a suspected data breach?

## Edge cases (should admit uncertainty)

- What is our Q3 revenue target? *(not in knowledge base)*
- Who is the CEO's favorite restaurant? *(not in knowledge base)*
