# Remote Work Policy

**Department:** HR  
**Last updated:** 2026-01-15  
**Tags:** remote, hybrid, workplace

## Overview

InsightFlow Inc. supports flexible remote and hybrid work arrangements for eligible employees.

## Eligibility

- Full-time employees after 90 days of employment
- Roles classified as remote-eligible in the job description
- Manager approval required

## Hybrid schedule

- Minimum 2 days per week in office for hybrid roles (Tuesday–Thursday encouraged)
- Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in your local timezone

## Equipment

- Company provides laptop, monitor stipend ($500 one-time), and VPN access
- Employees must use company-approved MDM on all devices accessing internal systems

## Annual leave while remote

Remote work does not change leave policies. Request annual leave through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for periods longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Security

- Never share credentials; use SSO only
- Lock screen when away; enable full-disk encryption
- Report lost devices within 1 hour to security@insightflow.example.com
