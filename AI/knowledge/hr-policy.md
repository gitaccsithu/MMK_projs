# HR Policy — Leave & Benefits

**Department:** HR  
**Tags:** leave, benefits, pto

## Annual leave

- 20 days PTO per year (pro-rated for new hires)
- Accrual begins on day 1; usable after 30 days
- Submit requests in Workday; manager approval required
- Maximum carry-over: 5 days into next calendar year

## Sick leave

- 10 sick days per year, no carry-over
- For absences over 3 days, a doctor's note may be requested

## Parental leave

- 16 weeks paid primary caregiver leave
- 8 weeks paid secondary caregiver leave

## Public holidays

InsightFlow observes 11 company holidays. Remote employees receive holiday pay per local employment contract.

## How to request leave

1. Open Workday → Time Off
2. Select leave type and dates
3. Add optional note for your manager
4. Submit at least 48 hours before start (2 weeks for 5+ days)
