# Onboarding Guide — New Hires

**Department:** HR  
**Tags:** onboarding, new-hire

## Week 1 checklist

- Day 1: IT setup (laptop, Okta, email, Slack)
- Day 1: HR orientation (benefits, policies)
- Day 2: Meet your manager and buddy
- Day 3: Security training (required LMS module)
- Day 5: Team intro sessions

## Accounts you will receive

- Okta SSO
- Google Workspace
- Slack
- Workday (HR)
- InsightFlow internal wiki

## Buddy program

Each new hire is assigned a buddy for 30 days for informal questions.

## 30-60-90 day goals

Work with your manager to document goals in Workday by end of week 2.

## Questions

Contact onboarding@insightflow.example.com or your HR business partner.
