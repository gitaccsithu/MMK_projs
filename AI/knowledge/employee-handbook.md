# Employee Handbook

**Department:** HR  
**Tags:** handbook, culture, conduct

## Mission

InsightFlow builds AI-powered knowledge tools for enterprise teams.

## Code of conduct

- Treat colleagues with respect
- No harassment or discrimination — zero tolerance
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR

## Working hours

- Standard hours: 9 AM – 5 PM local time, flexible with manager agreement
- Overtime requires pre-approval for non-exempt roles

## Expenses

Submit expenses within 30 days via Expensify. Receipts required over $25.

## Whistleblower

Report ethics concerns anonymously via ethics@insightflow.example.com or the third-party hotline listed on the intranet.
